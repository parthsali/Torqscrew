Pune Institute of Computer Technology, (PICT) is an engineering college in Pune, India. Established
by the Society for Computer Research and Training in 1983, it was the first institute to introduce a
Computer Engineering branch among private colleges in the Maharashtra state. It is ranked
amongst the topmost private engineering colleges in the country.

About Us

Established in Apr'21 PICT Automobile Club aims to promote motor sport's culture.
Welcome aboard for an electrifying ride! We are a group of enthusiastic self-taught technocrats, putting together our collective knowledge of different engineering disciplines such as mechanical, electronics, electrical, computers; in-order to design and construct an electric performance vehicle, and that too completely in-house!
